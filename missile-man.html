Missile man of INDIA

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, often called the Missile Man of India, was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India's missile development and nuclear weapons programs, earning him the title. His contributions to the Agni and Prithvi missile projects helped strengthen India's defense capabilities.
